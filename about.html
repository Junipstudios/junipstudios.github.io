About Me
I was born on August 31st, 2003. It was in my early childhood that I was diagnosed with Aperger's syndrome and ADHD. So it was no surprise I'd have my hyperfixations and special interests. This is part of why I went to college for New Media. I saw my dad do many things in media including commentating, news, and even website development. I would discover a year ago that I wanted to be a part of this program. They even accepted my name change! That's when I knew this was home, and I've never looked back since.
