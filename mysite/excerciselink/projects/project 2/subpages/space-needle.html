Space Needle

Welcome to the biggest landmark in Seattle and one of the biggest landmarks in America!

The Space Needle started being built in 1961, and only took 400 days to complete! Many call this the 400 day wonder because of that impressive building time.

For the New Years Celebration in 2021, There was a virtual light show at the Space Needle. Just goes to show after 60 Years this landmark is still absolutely stunning!

Believe it or not, there are actually place to sit and eat atop the Space Needle! Also plenty of great views as well.
