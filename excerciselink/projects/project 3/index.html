FLCC New Media

With FLCCs New Media degree, you not only learn the multiple aspects of technology needed to keep up in fast paced digital world, but also are pushed toward creative thinking in a fun relaxed state of the art environment

What can you do with New Media? Just about everything.

The New Media program at FLCC is designed to provide you with skills in animation, computer programming, video production, graphic design, interactive development, digital photography, social media and audio design. What will you do?

Get Started

You will also have opportunities to explore development for mobile and web deployment. With a New Media A.S degree you will be able to look for jobs in many different technology and creative fields, along with the ability to transfer to four year schools
